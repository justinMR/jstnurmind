About JSTNURMIND

JSTNURMIND (pronounced 'Just In Your Mind') is a red masked multi-genre music producer artist; styles range between alternative hip-hop, rap, house, and techno. Lyrics and music dance between feelings of reflection, pensiveness, playful spirits, upbeat swings, emotive melodies, and rhythmic hypnotic moods.

Originally from New York City.
Currently based in Austin, Texas.
